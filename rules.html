School Rules

1. Behavior in Class

  • Late more than 10 mins → warning.
  • Disruptive behavior → removed from class.
  • Using phone → confiscation + call to parents.
  • No materials → removed from class.
  • Doodling in notebook → removed from class.

2. Homework

  • Missed homework → allowed next lesson; then parents called.
  • Late homework → minus 2 points.
  • Copied answers → 1 point only.
  • Struggling students → moved to lower group.

3. Supplies

  • Folder or backpack required.
  • Small snack and water allowed.
  • Pen and notebook are mandatory.
  • Bring pen, notepad, English journal.
  • No colorful or explicit (+18) bags allowed.
